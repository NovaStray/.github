# RealQuick - Web-based Automated Scheduling Platform

RealQuick is an all-in-one user experience that provides advanced scheduling, a simple todo list, and insightful analytics all wrapped around with our Collective Dashboard Interface. With this design, you can access all your core needs while never leaving a single page!

## Features
- **Advanced Scheduling**: Autogenerate your timetabled schedule based on your preferences
- **Task Management**: Easily manage your workflow with our minimalistic interface
- **TimeBlock Management**: Quickly set up your daily routine such as gym, class, and meetings
- **Simple Settings**: No need to spend an hour customizing your preferences
- **Tagging System**: "Highlight" your tasks and timeblocks with unique colors and labels
- **Notes**: Add detailed notes to all your tasks and timeblocks
- **Filtering**: Filter data by view and by type
- **Schedule Optimization**: Automatically reorganize your timestamps for better workflows
- **Schedule Simulation**: Simulate permuatations of your settings to achieve an optimized schedule
- **Personalized Analytics**: Get personalized analytics based on your work habits and activites
- **Collective Dashboard Interface**: Access everything from a single page while maintaining minimalism
